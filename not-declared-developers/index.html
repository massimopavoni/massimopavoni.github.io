Not Declared Developers

Not Declared Developers is a project whose idea is to build a place to reunite people from various fields and different backgrounds, to share ideas and experiences, discuss technologies, promote open source projects and have fun altogether.

The first sketchy idea of this project formed back in February 2017 with a simple friendship between the original founders, who now are finally making it a reality, through the official Discord server.

So, if you sometimes feel the need of talking about some interesting nerdy or geeky information technology stuff you're passionate about, or if you simply want someone to share your programming achievements with, or if you seek advice instead, come and join the server, and maybe also take a look at the projects that members are working on.
